Hip Muscles
1

After completing this module, you will be able to:
Name the attachments and function of muscles controlling the hip joint
Name the muscles of the 6 deep lateral rotators group
Describe the relationship between joint movements, muscle shortening and muscle function for muscles at the hip
Identify muscles in cross-sections of the body

Muscles are needed to flex/extend, abduct/adduct and medially/laterally rotate the hip
